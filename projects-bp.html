About the Program

BP Fellowship Program

Advancing global research excellence in Korea

The Brain Pool Fellowship Program invites outstanding international scientists to work with Korean universities, research institutes, and industry partners where advanced expertise is most needed. Through collaborative research, the program is designed to generate high-impact outcomes, promote the internationalization of Korea’s research environment, cultivate emerging talent, and expand enduring global research networks.

Urban AI Institute

Building the analytical foundation for next-generation urban intelligence

The Urban AI Institute aims to establish an Urban AI technology framework capable of quantitatively diagnosing and anticipating complex urban problems. It develops simulation structures for analyzing interactions among population dynamics, mobility patterns, and environmental conditions, while laying the groundwork for algorithms and models that enable policy experimentation. Through this approach, the institute seeks to build a long-term research foundation for addressing climate risk, urban inequality, and compound urban disasters.

Research Domains

Geospatial AI

Develops Urban AI models for diagnosing social imbalance through synthetic population generation, LLM-based mobility forecasting, and urban air mobility site analysis, with a focus on spatial dynamics, accessibility, and uneven urban conditions.

Lead Faculty: Prof. Yoonjin Yoon

Climate AI

Builds analytical frameworks for assessing disaster vulnerability in urban physical infrastructure, predicting spatially differentiated climate risks, and evaluating infrastructure investment and policy scenarios for resilient urban adaptation.

Lead Faculty: Prof. Soh Young In

Physical AI

Integrates distributed autonomous infrastructure control with large-scale agent-based modeling to connect physical urban systems with intelligent decision-making architectures for complex and adaptive urban environments.

Lead Faculty: Prof. Soojean Han
